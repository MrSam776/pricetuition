I'm Samuel, a maths tutor based in London, working with students preparing for SATs and GCSE examinations.

My background is in accounting and finance, which means I approach maths the way it should be: logically, systematically, and with a clear reason behind every concept. I've found that most students who struggle with maths aren't struggling with the subject itself — they're struggling with how it's been explained to them.

I've been tutoring since 2022, and the approach I've landed on is simple. I have a younger brother ten years my junior, and that relationship shapes how I work with students — approachable, patient, and straight-talking. No jargon, no condescension. Just someone in their corner who actually gets it.

I offer one-to-one sessions in person across London and online. Every session is built around where the student actually is, not where the curriculum assumes they should be.
